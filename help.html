The Clock Help

Alarms

Set, Modify or Cancel Alarms

This section explains how to set, modify or cancel alarms.

1. Open The Clock app.

2. At the top, tap Alarm

3. Pick an alarm

-To add an alarm, tap ADD icon ()

-To modify an alarm, tap alarm

4. Enter alarm settings

5. Tap save button ()

6. Set alarm settings

-Choose alarm time from timepicker

-Enter alarm name

-Set repeating days if desired

-Select sound type SILENT, RINGTONE, MUSIC or ONLINE RADIO

-Select tone

-Select snooze option

-Set maximum alarm volume

-To have an alarm become louder over time, tap Gradually increase volume.

-Set vibration. Tap to choose vibration pattern or set vibration to OFF.

-Check if you would like to play sound in silent mode

-Choose an option for an alarm to require solving math problems to stop

-Select sound type -> ONLINE RADIO

-Tap Ringtone

-Tap add button () to add an online radio

-Enter name of the radio

-Enter URL of online radio

Note:The online radio streaming URL is not the same as the main URL of the radio station website. For most of the radio stations, you can get it from the original radio player app or a website.

If you want to edit the online radio details or remove it from the list completely, tap and hold the radio name, so the Edit and Delete options appear. Then tap the pencil or trash icon to perform your preferred action.

Note:You need an internet connection to play online radios. If the connection fails during alarm time, the default alarm tone will be played.

-To cancel an alarm, switch off alarm by tapping the switch button

-To delete an alarm, tap trash icon ()

When your alarm goes off, you can delay or stop it. If the alarm is set to repeat, it will still go off in the future.

Snooze:To delay an alarm tap snooze button

Stop:To stop an alarm tap and HOLD stop button

You can change your snooze time from the alarm settings.

In order to add an Alarm Clock Widget to your home screen;

Add widgets:Go to widgets from your device. Choose: The Clock widgets. Tap and hold desired widget and move it to your home screen

Choose an alarm to display on widget.

To activate or de-activate and alarm;

Tap CLOCK icon.

In order to add a Nap Timer widget to your home screen;

Add widgets:Go to widgets from your device. Choose: The Clock widgets. Tap and hold Nap Timer widget and move it to your home screen

- Set Nap Time.

- Modify alarm settings.

- Tap SAVE button ().

World Clock

World Clock displays the current time in selected cities

This section explains how to add or remove cities to world clock.

Add a city: Tap ADD button (). Select a city to add.

Remove a city: Tap on TRASH icon ()

Times displayed are exact current times, taking into account DST (Daylight Savings Time) times.

In order to add a World Clock widget to your home screen;

Add widgets:Go to widgets from your device. Choose The Clock widgets. Tap and hold desired widget and move it to your home screen

The first three cities are displayed for triple World Clock widgets.

You can change the clock format from the settings window;

Open Settings:Tap SETTINGS icon () on top right of the app

Tap Clock Format and choose 12 Hours or 24 Hours.

Timer

You can count time down to zero with the timer.

This section explains how to add a timer or manage timers.

To start a timer;

- Enter hours, minutes and seconds to numpad.

- Tap START button.

You can add a maximum of 10 timers by pressing the ADD button ().

Pause: Tap PAUSE button.

Resume: Tap RESUME button.

You can add one minute anytime while a countdown timer is running.

To delete a timer tap delete icon ()

To add a Countdown Timer to your home screen;

Add widget: Go to widgets from your device. Select: The Clock Widgets

Tap and hold Countdown Timer widget and move it to your home screen.

Enter hours, minutes and seconds from the numpad and tap START button.

To Start Countdown Timer:

Tap PLAY button.

To Pause Countdown Timer:

Tap PAUSE button.

To Reset Countdown Timer:

Tap RESET button.

To change timer settings tap SETTINGS icon () on top right of the app.

Ringtone: Select a tone to play when timer goes off.

Volume: Adjust timer alarm volume.

Increase Volume Gradually: Tap to choose if you would like to have timer alarm volume increasing in time.

Vibrate: Tap to choose vibration pattern or set vibration to OFF.

Play in silent mode: Switch on to play timer alarm even when the device is in silent mode

Stopwatch

You can count time up from zero with the stopwatch.

This section explains how to run and manage Stopwatch.

Start your Stopwatch:

Tap START button.

To Pause Stopwatch:

Tap PAUSE button.

To Resume Stopwatch:

Tap RESUME button.

To add a Lap:

Tap LAP button.

To Reset Stopwatch:

Tap RESET button.

To share recorded lap times;

Tap SHARE (): Choose an application to share lap times

To Modify Stopwatch settings;

Tap SETTINGS icon (): Scroll down to Stopwatch settings

Keep screen on: Switch on to keep screen on while Stopwatch is running

Use volume buttons for laps: Switch on to use your device volume buttons for lap recording

Bedside Clock

You can display current time and alarm information on retro-style Bedside Clock via Android's Daydream function.

Your device must be on a docking station or plugged in to display bedside clock.

- Open your device settings.

- Choose Screen Saver (or Daydream).

- Select: The Clock.

- Save Settings.
